CREDITS — Backstories Anthology
=======================================================================

This mod is a unified merge of multiple community-created backstories
mods for RimWorld. All original work remains the property of their
respective authors. This merge is provided as a free, non-commercial
compilation to enhance the RimWorld experience.

NOTES
-----------------------------------------------------------------------

- All backstory defNames have been prefixed with 'UB_' to ensure
  uniqueness and prevent conflicts between any other mods.
- The Elderhood backstory system (DLL + Patches) has been integrated
  directly into this mod's assemblies underneath the 1.5/ and 1.6/ folders.
- Childhood Backstories (Zylle) uses custom ZCB.ZCBackstoryDef XML type
  registered by the included ZCB.dll. DefNames prefixed UB_ZCB_ with
  spawnCategories ZCB/ZCB_NaturalMeditation/ZCB_Placeholder intact.
  10 custom RecordDefs track childhood activities. ZCB.dll in 1.5/1.6 Assemblies.
- PlaceDefs from Seal's Collection are included under Defs/PlaceDefs/ and
  are used by RimWorld's procedural backstory generation system.
- Seal's Collection: All four versions (v1.0-v1.3) are included with
  disjoint defNames — no conflicts between versions. Total: 137 backstories.
- Medieval Backstories was converted from AlienRace.AlienBackstoryDef to
  standard BackstoryDef for maximum compatibility (no Alien Races required).
- Reasonable Moods & Capable Backstories patches have been applied directly
  to backstory definitions: workDisables removed where nonsensical, skill
  penalties added instead. The DLL provides mood balancing features.
- ALL PATCHES USE UB_ PREFIXES — no conflicts with original mods.
- TOTAL BACKSTORIES IN THIS MOD: 1,352
- This mod is 100% standalone - none of the 13 original source mods
  are required for it to function.
- See individual mod pages on the Steam Workshop for more details
  about each collection.

If any author wishes to have their work removed or modified, please
contact the maintainer.
